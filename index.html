France's underground water sources

This website shows France's underground water source, their qualities, and specific risks and pollutants.
This map shows the water bodies and colors them according to their quality. For more details about composition, click on the water source.
This visualization is for local inhabitants that wants to check the quality of water next to where they live. Afterall, it's important to drink clean water that won't endanger your health.
